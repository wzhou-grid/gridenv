About Us

Grid Environment Ltd., founded in 2015 and headquartered in Calgary, Alberta, is a Canadian environmental consulting firm proudly serving clients for over years. We specialize in Leak Detection and Repair (LDAR), emissions monitoring for oil, gas, and petrochemical facilities, air quality monitoring, soil and water sampling, and other environmental services.

In addition to field services, we developed LDAR Server, a powerful software platform designed to manage component inventories, track leaks, streamline regulatory reporting, and calculate emissions. LDAR Server offers a modern, user-friendly alternative to legacy systems and is built to meet the evolving needs of industrial operators and environmental professionals.

Our team brings together seasoned professionals, a comprehensive inventory of specialized equipment, and deep expertise in various LDAR software platforms. We are known for our strong communication skills, precise project and cost control, and commitment to working with and training local resources.

At Grid Environment, we combine technical excellence with practical solutions to help clients achieve regulatory compliance and reduce their environmental impact efficiently and effectively.
